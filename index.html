Defination

The Upanishads are part of a large collection of sacred books of Hindu people, called the Vedas. They deal with knowledge or wisdom.Upanishads are the philosophical-religious texts of Hinduism also known as Sanatan Dharma meaning "Eternal Order". The literally meaning of upanishads is "approaching and sitting near" they contain conversation between teachers and students most of the ideas wre represented as dialogues.
Types of soul

2. Brahman meaning universal soul.
